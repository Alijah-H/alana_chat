About ALANA Chat

RIT contains numerous groups that ensure ALANA (African, Latin, Asian, Native American) students have access to opportunities and are in a position to flourish. Students often find the abundance of resources overwhelming and hard to track down. ALANA Chat is a student-created tool for sharing information and navigating resources.

Created in 2022 as a capstone project, ALANA Chat has been passed down through several groups of Humanities, Computing, and Design students passionate about helping ALANA students thrive on RIT’s diverse campus.
