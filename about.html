About



            RIT contains numerous groups that ensure ALANA (African, Latin, 
            Asian, Native American) students have access to opportunities and 
            are in a position to flourish. However, students report that the 
            abundance of distinct resources and groups are overwhelming and 
            hard to track down, leaving them unsure of which opportunities to
             seize and which communities to engage with. ALANA Chat is a student 
            created and operated tool for sharing information and navigating 
            resources. 

            Created in 2022 as a capstone project, ALANA Chat has 
            been passed down through several groups of Humanities, Computing, 
            and Design students passionate about helping ALANA students thrive 
            and flourish on RIT’s diverse campus.
